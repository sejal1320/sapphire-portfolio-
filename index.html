    HARI OM THALASSIC

    Established in 2022, Hari Om Thalassic is a rapidly growing maritime venture dedicated to supporting seafarers throughout their professional journey. The organization serves as a reliable partner for merchant navy professionals by offering guidance, documentation support, training assistance, and industry connections.

    Our Services

    • Comprehensive support related to maritime training and documentation.
    • Career guidance for aspiring and existing seafarers.
    • End-to-end support at every stage of a seafarer's career.
    • Assistance with documentation requirements throughout their professional journey.
    • Support for rank upgradation of existing seafarers and crew members in the merchant navy.

    Industry Network

    • Acts as a single point of contact for seafarers with an extensive network of merchant navy training institutes across Mumbai and Navi Mumbai.
    • Collaborates with training institutes and maritime organizations across India.
    • Maintains partnerships with over 30 agencies across Pan India.

    Team & Impact

    • Powered by a dedicated team of 20–30 members working tirelessly for the welfare of crew members.
